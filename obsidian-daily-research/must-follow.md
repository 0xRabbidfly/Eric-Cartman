# Must-Follow Accounts

> Every tweet from these accounts is captured — no engagement filter, no quality
> filter. Grouped by label for the daily note.
>
> **Format:** `- @handle — Display Name`
> Lines starting with `#` are group headers. Blank lines and comments (`>`) are ignored.
> To disable an account temporarily, comment it out with `>`.
