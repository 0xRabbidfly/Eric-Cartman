Key Observations

  • Outbacks are scarce privately. Only 1 qualifying private Outback within 250km (Hamilton, $28,550). Most inventory is dealer on AutoTrader.

  • Rogue offers 12% more cargo space (36.5 vs 32.5 cu ft) and generally lower prices for equivalent year/mileage.

  • 2021+ Rogues are new generation with improved interior, 1.5L turbo (2022+), and better tech. They represent strong value vs older models at similar prices.
